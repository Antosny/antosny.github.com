GENDER TRAITS TEST [aka "the Bem Test"]

This is an online interactive magical calculating Gender Traits Test (aka Bem Test)!

Disclaimer: This test purports to decide how masculine or feminine you are, based on the paper linked above. The paper is interesting, as it took a bunch of people (male and female) and asked them which characteristics were preferred, and this is the result. I am very wary of any assigning gender like this, but our society has decided that some characteristics are "masculine" and others are "feminine", and if we avoid assigning those to "male" and "female", this test may or may not be interesting, but hopefully won't be too harmful.

This is a dual-purpose form! If you feel so inclined, you can print out things and fill stuff out and use a calculator, OR you can just pick boxes and hit the magic button. The people over at the original test evidently have a very limited definition of "interactive", which consists of choosing numbers from boxes. Mine is more comprehensive, which means that this page will take the fantastically complex step of adding and subtracting numbers for you, and then, on top of that, choosing one of five labels, based on that final number! I know, I know, I am a wizard of modern technology, but please don't let it frighten you. It's not entirely witchcraft.

Answer the questions as to how the term describes you best according to the following scale:

1 = Never or almost never true 5 = Often true
2 = Usually not true 6 = Usually true
3 = Sometimes but infrequently true 7 = Always or almost always true
4 = Occasionally true

How the scoring works (in case you want to do it yourself for some reason):

Total the second and third columns. The first column is what might be called a dummy column in order to keep you from skewing the test while you're taking it.

Total from the second column is Score A:
Total from the third column is Score B:
Subtract Score B from Score A for the Difference Score:

For instance,
if your A score is 90 and your B score is 70, it would be 90-70=+20 (positive 20);
if your A score is 70 and your B score is 90, it would be 70-90=-20 (negative 20).
